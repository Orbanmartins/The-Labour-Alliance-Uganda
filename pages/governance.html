GOVERNANCE

There are two major components of governance at the Labour Alliance Uganda and that is the board of directors and the Secretariat.

MESSAGE FROM THE DIRECTOR

"Unemployment in our country should not be an excuse for excessive gross labour exploitation. We will track, document and make available cases of labour violations inside and outside the country. If we don't document them, even when government agencies are there, victims will not be ready to claim redress even when they are represented"

At the Labour Alliance the board of directors is the highest governing body and is responsible for the overall well-being of the Alliance. Acting within its official mandate, the Board has all rights to recruit and terminate, and evaluates the Executive Secretary and all other members of the Alliance and equally helps the Alliance to fulfill its vision, mission, and values, sets strategic direction and monitor progress, ensure the fiscal health of the Alliance. Board members have a mandate to take on specific tasks on behalf of the Alliance from time to time, but in general they are not involved in the day-to-day implementation of services but can be called upon where necessary.
