LIVE STOCK HEALTH INSURANCE

  • Image Holder
  • Services
  • Products
  • Company

Livestock sector is an important sector of our nation, especially in the rural economy. The supplemental income derived from rearing of livestock is a great source of support to the farmers facing uncertainties of crop production apart from providing sustenance to poor and landless famers. For protection of the livestock sector, the labour alliance Uganda has embarked on livestock health insurance scheme that is taking off in the districts of Kiruhura, Isingiro, Sembabule and Masindi on a pilot basis of a five year plan.. The scheme will be implemented on a regular basis in 12 selected districts of the country. Under the scheme, all cattle are being insured at maximum of their current market price. The premium of the insurance is subsidized to the tune of 50 percent. Livestock insurance scheme has been formulated with the twin objective of providing protection mechanism to the farmers and cattle rearing communities against any eventual loss due to poor disease control and management, poor quality farm inputs and lack of proper scientific advice. Our insurance is unique in nature because it is designed to demonstrate the benefit of the insurance of livestock to the people with an ultimate goal of attaining qualitative improvement in livestock and their products. The scheme was started with the primary goal of providing more effective steps for disease control and improvement of genetic quality of animals for promotion of livestock sector. .
