WORKPLACE HEALTH AND SAFETY

  • Image Holder
  • Services
  • Products
  • Company

The department of workplace health and safety has a wider coverage but its main purpose is to save lives, prevent workplace injuries and illnesses, and protect the health of all Uganda's workers. This includes efforts to protect groups of workers who are small and unorganized but who are particularly vulnerable or who face special hazards. Whenever possible, this component will seek and expect implementation of hazard control strategies based upon primary prevention, i.e., strategies which focus on fixing the underlying causes of problems and reducing hazardous exposures at their source. The department moves from inspection, training and stretches to initiate strategic, public-private partnerships to identify and encourage the spread of industry best practices to solve national problems. At The Labour Alliance health and safety department, employer commitment and meaningful employee participation and involvement in safety and health is a key ingredient our effective programs. They make sure that all safety and health services, resources, rules, and information must be readily accessible and understandable to employees, employers, and all industrial and construction staff. The Labour Alliance through the department of Workplace Health and Safety intends to be a performance-oriented, data-driven organization that places the highest premium on real results rather than activities and processes. The Labour Alliance Uganda programs must be judged according to their success at eliminating hazards and reducing injuries and illnesses at workplace. .
